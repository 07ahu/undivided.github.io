Affordable Connectivity Program

ACP provides internet services and devices to those who qualify. Qualfications include:

  • Household income below 200% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines
  • Received a Federal Pell Grant during the current award year
  • Particpates in:
    • Free and Reduced-Price School Lunch Program or School Breakfast Program
    • SNAP
    • Medicaid
    • Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) Program
    • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
    • WIC
    • Veterans Pension or Survivor Benefits
    • Lifeline
More qualifications and information can be found here. Different internet providers are participating in the Affordable Connectivity Program and you can see a large list of providers in Maryland here or you can see some in the list below. Apply to these programs listed and you can get free internet!

Lifeline Phone Service

Lifeline is a government benefit that provides free cell phone service in Maryland for low-income individuals and families. Go to here to see if you qualify and enroll now to get a free phone and data. If you have questions call 410.767.8000 or go to the Baltimore Office at William Donald Schaefer Tower 6 St. Paul St., 16th Floor Baltimore, MD 21202.

Clear Wireless

Apply to get a free phone with unlimited data, a free hotspot, or $10.99 tablet. It is one offer per household and you have to qualify. Qualifying criteria can be found at https://www.clearwireless.com/ Start by applying at https://apply.clearwireless.com/

Cintex Wireless

Those who qualify by program or income can receive a free phone with an unlimited plan. Start by applying now at https://cintexwireless.com/apply or find more info here.

TruConnect

If eligible, you'll get up to 14 GB of monthly data and a free Android phone or Wi-Fi hotspot. Start by enrolling now or finding more information at https://www.truconnect.com/

Gen Mobile

Eligible customers will receive a free Gen Mobile 7GB plan and smartphone through the Affordable Connectivity Program. Start by applying at here.

Assurance Wireless

Get a free android smartphone, unlimited data, texts, and minutes. Learn about their service at https://www.assurancewireless.com/lifeline-services/what-lifeline Check if their service is in your area here and then start your application.

Xfinity

Sign up for Internet Essentials for only $9.95/month with no annual contract and free equipment – then enroll in the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) to get your service for FREE. Learn more here.
